Marcy Gross, Dance Instructor

Marcy Gross is a fabulous dancer and dance instructor for Swing and much more. This site uses a custom design that incorporates Marcy's favorite colors. It is an example of how content from ExploreDance.com can be integrated into other sites. Marcy also writes for ExploreDance.com. The special programming used in her site creates a list of her ExploreDance.com articles that automatically updates every time a new article is posted.
