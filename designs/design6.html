Joseph Patelson Music House

Joseph Patelson Music House is one of the leading retailers for sheet music. We helped them kick start the development of their existing web site, and served as their webmaster until they were ready to have their own staff assume responsibilities for day to day updating. In the course of creating new sections of the site, we developed a set of electronic books based on Patelson publications that date from before the advent of the personal computer. These electronic books have not been released for sale at this time, but for a small investment, Patelson was able to give itself the option of selling electronic products in the future. We also set up a Constant Contact email account for Patelson. We continue to provide technical advice on the Patelson website and emails as needed.
