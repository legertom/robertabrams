Jake's Saloon

Jake's Saloon at 23rd Street was a custom designed site for a sports bar and restaurant. We developed the graphic design for the site with major assistance from Tom Kirrane. Most of the photography and HTML implementation featured in the site was by Robert Abrams. Tom took Robert's photography and created graphic elements from details of the restaurant's décor, such as their beautiful lamps. The result is a site as hip as the Chelsea neighborhood in with the restaurant is located. If you want to cheer on your favorite team while partaking of hearty grub and excellent beer, check out Jake's Saloon.
We also provided some updates to Jake's Saloon's existing Flash based site for their 57th street restaurant.
