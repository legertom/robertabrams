Concept Mapping

Concept mapping is a technique for representing the structure of knowledge. We have used it to help people understand topics as diverse as science and religious tolerance. We have used concept maps to help people express and organize their knowledge, as well as to assess their knowledge, documenting growth over time. We have used the technique in schools, non-profits and business settings. We have developed the LifeMap concept mapping software for the Macintosh, and can also assist with the use of programs such as CMapTools and Inspiration. If you are trying to get a grip on complex problems, let us help you give concept mapping a try.
