Education Research

We have assisted schools with research and evaluation. We have experience working with public and private schools, both in New York and California. We primarily focus on educational technology, such as the implementation of laptop computers in schools. We have also worked with curriculum developers, such as the developers of a CD-rom curriculum on Environmental Health and indoor air quality. This evaluation used a combination of surveys, interviews and classroom observations to help the company improve both the software and the use of the software with the goal of taking the curriculum to market. If you need an outside perspective to help improve an educational technology project, contact us to see how we might be able to assist you.
